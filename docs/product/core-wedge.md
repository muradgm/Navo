# Core Wedge

## The Weak Generic Version

> A trip planner that helps people plan trips.

This is too broad and too weak.

## Stronger Wedge

> TripOS helps travelers decide what to do next by turning their real trip constraints into a practical plan.

## Sharpest Current Wedge

> The best next trip decision, personalized to your context.

## Why This Matters

Most travel products are organized around places.

TripOS should be organized around decisions:

- Is this worth doing today?
- Is it too far with kids?
- Is it open?
- Does it fit the weather?
- Does it fit the budget?
- Can we get there easily?
- What should we do if we only have 3 hours?
- What should we skip?
- What should we do near our apartment/hotel?

## Product Principle

A suggestion is only useful if it respects the user’s trip context.

## Positioning Line Options

1. Your trip, turned into a daily operating plan.
2. A travel planner that thinks with your real constraints.
3. Know what to do next, wherever your trip takes you.
4. The personal trip companion for families, tourists, and business travelers.
5. Not more places. Better decisions.
