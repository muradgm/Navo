# Personas

## Family Tourist

Example: parent traveling with children for 3–5 days.

Needs:
- avoid overplanning
- kid-friendly activities
- food certainty
- nearby parks
- public transport simplicity
- weather backup plan

Success:
- no daily decision fatigue
- kids remain engaged
- spending stays under control

## Business Traveler

Example: consultant in a city for meetings with free evening blocks.

Needs:
- 2-hour plans
- dinner near hotel/client site
- quiet work cafés
- transport reliability
- low-risk recommendations

Success:
- uses dead time well
- avoids tourist traps
- returns to hotel/airport on time

## Short-Stop Visitor

Example: 6-hour layover or train stop.

Needs:
- luggage-aware plan
- station/airport route
- compressed city highlights
- strict timing

Success:
- sees the best practical option without risking departure.

## Local Explorer

Example: resident looking for weekend ideas.

Needs:
- weather-aware suggestions
- family options
- hidden gems
- free/cheap activities

Success:
- discovers something useful without planning friction.
