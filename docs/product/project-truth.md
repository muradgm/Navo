# Project Truth

## Working Name

TripOS / CarryTrip / Travel Companion

Final naming is not locked. The documents use **TripOS** as a temporary internal name.

## Product Truth

TripOS is not just a travel itinerary builder.

TripOS is a personal trip companion that helps a traveler make better decisions before and during a trip by combining:

- trip dates and time windows
- arrival/departure details
- accommodation/base location
- traveler type
- family/business/solo context
- budget
- food preferences
- weather
- public transport and walking reality
- opening hours and day constraints
- energy level
- nearby essentials
- emergency/practical information

The product should feel like something the traveler takes with them, not something they use once while planning.

## Core Wedge

> The best next trip decision, personalized to your context.

Expanded:

> TripOS turns your trip facts and constraints into a live, practical operating plan: what to do, when to do it, how to get there, what it costs, and what to avoid.

## What It Is

- a trip operating system
- a personalized planner
- a day-by-day decision assistant
- a context-aware activity finder
- a family/business/solo companion
- a budget-aware travel guide
- a weather-aware day planner
- a local essentials guide

## What It Is Not

- not a generic list of tourist attractions
- not a booking marketplace first
- not another static itinerary PDF
- not a city guide blog
- not a travel inspiration-only app
- not a map clone
- not an AI chatbot with no structured product surface

## Key Product Belief

Travel planning breaks because most tools ignore actual constraints.

Real travelers do not only ask:

> What can I do in Basel?

They ask:

> We arrive at 15:36, have kids, breakfast is included, one child gets tired, we eat halal/vegetarian, it may rain, we want to stay near the hotel today, and we only want to spend €80. What is the best plan now?

TripOS should answer that.

## Current Proof Example

The Basel family planner proved the direction:

- hotel/base-aware recommendations
- breakfast included impacts budget
- train arrival/departure affects itinerary
- kids and family energy matter
- halal/vegetarian food constraints matter
- weather changes the best plan
- a Saint-Louis tram mini-trip needs conditional details
- a Zürich half-day option needs budget/time warnings
- favorites and add-to-day behavior matter
- images and location cards improve engagement

The reusable product should extract this pattern and generalize it.
