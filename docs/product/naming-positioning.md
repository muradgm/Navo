# Naming and Positioning

## Naming Criteria

The name should suggest:
- a companion you carry
- smart daily decisions
- practical travel support
- context-aware planning
- not generic itinerary software

## Name Options

### CarryTrip

Meaning: the trip companion you carry with you.

Pros:
- simple
- consumer-friendly
- mobile feeling

Cons:
- less premium/business

### TripOS

Meaning: operating system for trips.

Pros:
- strong product metaphor
- good for serious planning
- business + leisure compatible

Cons:
- may feel technical

### DayPilot Travel

Meaning: guides each day.

Pros:
- clear utility
- traveler-friendly

Cons:
- less unique

### TravelBrief

Meaning: concise operational brief for each travel day.

Pros:
- good for business travelers

Cons:
- less family/emotional

### GoContext

Meaning: travel decisions based on context.

Pros:
- product logic in name

Cons:
- less immediately travel-specific

## Current Recommendation

Use **TripOS** internally.

For public brand exploration, test:

- CarryTrip
- DayPilot Travel
- TripOS
