# Basel Proof Case

## Why Basel Was Useful

The Basel family planner exposed the real product pattern:

- exact train times changed itinerary logic
- breakfast included changed budget logic
- family/kids changed pacing
- halal/vegetarian changed food planning
- weather needed dynamic logic
- Saint-Louis required conditional side-trip details
- Zürich half-day required optional expansion
- groceries mattered because apartment cooking mattered
- Add/favorites had to work clearly
- images improved the browsing experience

## Extracted Product Pattern

A trip planner becomes useful when it understands the full travel context, not just the destination.
