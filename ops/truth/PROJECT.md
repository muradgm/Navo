# TripOS Project

Last updated: 2026-06-08

## Product Truth

TripOS is a personalized trip companion that helps travelers decide what to do before and during a trip by combining dates, base location, traveler context, weather, transport, budget, food preferences, and time windows.

## Core Wedge

> The best next trip decision, personalized to your context.

## Current Prototype Origin

The product originated from a Basel family trip planner for:

- 2 adults
- 2 children
- dates 13.07–17.07
- train arrival/departure details
- hotel/base location
- breakfast included
- halal/vegetarian food preferences
- apartment dinner strategy
- Basel activities, parks, side trips, and Zürich option

This is a proof case, not the final product boundary.

## Current Product Direction

The product must become reusable for:

- tourists
- families
- business travelers
- short-stop visitors
- local explorers
- trips lasting hours, days, or weeks

## Current Non-Negotiables

- mobile-first
- context-aware
- practical, not generic
- dynamic weather capable
- clear budget impact
- base-location-aware
- food/preference-aware
- Add/favorite actions must work visibly
- no fake certainty for weather, opening hours, or transport

## MVP Product Surface

- Trip Setup
- Today Plan
- Activities
- Day Builder
- Weather
- Food & Essentials
- Budget
- Safety / Practical Info
- Favorites

## Technical Preference

Start with React + Vite + TypeScript.

Keep frontend/local-first until product behavior is validated.
